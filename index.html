Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build my website?

+

Most projects launch within 14 days. Simple sites can be ready in as little as 10 days, while more complex sites may take 3-4 weeks. We'll give you an exact timeline during our discovery call.

What if I don't like the design?

+

You get unlimited revisions during the design phase. We don't move to development until you're 100% happy. Plus, our satisfaction guarantee means you can get your deposit back if we can't align on a design you love.

Do I need to provide content and images?

+

We can work with whatever you have. If you need help with copywriting or professional photos, we can recommend partners or include content creation in your package for an additional fee.

What about hosting and maintenance?

+

We set up your site on fast, reliable hosting (usually around $20-50/year). We also offer optional maintenance plans starting at $99/month that include updates, backups, and minor changes.

Can I update the website myself?

+

Absolutely! Depending on your package, we can build your site with an easy-to-use content management system that lets you update text, images, and more without any coding knowledge.

What's the payment schedule?

+

We require 50% upfront to begin work, with the remaining 50% due before launch. For larger projects, we can set up a payment plan that works for your budget.
