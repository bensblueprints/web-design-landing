About This Service

What to Expect from Crowns & Bridges

When a tooth is cracked, severely decayed, or weakened by a large filling, a crown restores it to full strength and beauty. At First Dental, we use premium porcelain and zirconia materials that are custom-crafted to match your natural teeth in color, shape, and translucency. For missing teeth, bridges span the gap using adjacent teeth as anchors — restoring your bite and preventing neighboring teeth from shifting.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a crown?
The process typically requires two visits spaced about two weeks apart. The first visit is for preparation and impressions; the second is for permanent placement.
What is the difference between a crown and a bridge?
A crown covers a single damaged tooth. A bridge replaces one or more missing teeth by anchoring artificial teeth to crowns placed on the adjacent natural teeth.
How long do dental crowns last?
With good oral hygiene and regular checkups, dental crowns typically last 15 to 25 years. We monitor them at every visit and advise replacement if wear or issues develop.
